About the game

A very popular game played using your hands where the player makes shapes using their fingers to make a rock, paper or scissors.

The player who makes the superior hand sign wins that round.

The rules are -

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beat paper

In this computer simulation of this popular game you play against the computer for 5 consecutive rounds. The winner gets 1 point and draws give 0 points to both players. The Scorecard shows the points and the winner is decided at the end of all rounds.
